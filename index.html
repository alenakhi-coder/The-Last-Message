01The Story

The Journey

Experience an authentic and respectful retelling of one of history's most impactful stories

Ancient Mecca

Step into a fully explorable open world set in Mecca over 1400 years ago. Discover the city's environment, culture, and daily life before and during the early days of Islam, recreated with attention to historical atmosphere and detail.

Divine Revelation

Experience the beginning of a message that changed history. Follow key moments from the life of the Prophet and witness the early stages of revelation through immersive storytelling and meaningful interactions.

Trials & Growth

Take part in quests, challenges, and events inspired by real historical struggles. Progress through the game by completing objectives, exploring, and overcoming trials that reflect perseverance, patience, and faith.

Knowledge & Discovery

Unlock a rich codex system featuring collections of hadiths, historical insights, and important locations. Learn while you play through exploration, hidden discoveries, and interactive systems designed to both engage and educate.

02Knowledge

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about The Last Message

What is The Last Message about? ▼

The Last Message is an open-world game set in Mecca during the time of the Prophet. It combines exploration, storytelling, and interactive gameplay to immerse players in a historically inspired environment while discovering key moments, teachings, and characters.

Is the game historically accurate? ▼

The game is designed with strong inspiration from authentic historical and religious sources. While some gameplay elements are adapted for an interactive experience, the goal is to respect and reflect the historical context as accurately as possible.

What platforms will it be available on? ▼

The game is currently being developed for PC. Additional platforms may be considered in the future depending on the project's growth and community support.

How can I support the development? ▼

You can support the project by following development updates, sharing the game, joining the community on Discord, or supporting through platforms like Patreon. Every bit of support helps bring the project closer to completion.

When will the game be released? ▼

There is no official release date yet. The game is actively in development, and updates are shared regularly as progress continues. Early versions and tests may be available before the full release.

ARABIAN TRIBES

The Clans of Quraysh — 7th Century Arabia

◆ ◆ ◆
Mecca — 7th Century Arabia

The Quraysh were the dominant tribe of Mecca at the time of the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ, divided into two groups: the Quraysh of the Hollow (al-Bitah), dwelling near the Ka'bah, and the Quraysh of the Outskirts (al-Zawahir). The Prophet ﷺ himself descended from the Bani Hashim clan — Quraysh of the Hollow.
